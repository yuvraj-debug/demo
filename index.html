Rules & FAQ

What are the eligibility requirements?

Players must be at least 16 years old. Each team must consist of 5 main players and up to 2 substitutes. All players must have accounts in good standing with no current bans in the respective game.

What is the tournament schedule?

Tournaments run from Friday to Sunday each week. Group stages are on Friday, quarter and semi-finals on Saturday, and the grand finals on Sunday. All times are in UTC.

How are prizes distributed?

Prizes are distributed within 30 days after the tournament concludes. The winning team receives 50%, second place 30%, and third place 20% of the prize pool. Payment is made via PayPal or bank transfer.

What are the rules regarding substitutions?

Teams can substitute players between matches but not during a match. Each substitution must be reported to tournament officials at least 1 hour before the match begins.
